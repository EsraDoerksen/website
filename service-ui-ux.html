UI / UX Strategy

Designs That Turn Visitors
Into Loyal Customers

Research-driven user experience strategy and interface design that eliminates friction, builds trust, and makes every interaction feel effortless — and every click count.

Our Objectives

What We Aim to Achieve

Great UI/UX is not about aesthetics alone — it's about outcomes. These four objectives guide every design decision we make.

Conversion Rate Uplift

Systematically remove every barrier between your visitor and your goal — whether that's a purchase, enquiry, sign-up, or call. A 1% CRO improvement compounds dramatically at scale.

User Retention & Loyalty

Experiences people want to return to. Intuitive flows, consistent design language, and micro-interactions that delight — making your product the one users recommend to others.

Brand Coherence

A unified visual language across every touchpoint — website, app, email, and social — that builds recognition and trust with every interaction your customers have with your brand.

Inclusive Accessibility

WCAG 2.1 AA compliant design that works for every user — regardless of ability, device, or context. Accessibility is not a constraint; it's a marker of quality and commercial reach.

Our Approach

How We Design for Impact

Design without research is decoration. Our process is evidence-based, iterative, and always anchored in the real behaviour of real users.

01

User Research & Insights

We talk to your users, analyse your analytics, and run heuristic evaluations of your existing experience. This research phase surfaces the real friction points — not just the ones that seem obvious — and defines the opportunity space for design.

User interviews & qualitative research
Analytics & heatmap analysis (GA4, Hotjar)
Heuristic UX evaluation & competitor benchmarking
02

Strategy & Information Architecture

Before opening a design tool, we define the information architecture, user flows, and content hierarchy. A clear IA ensures users always know where they are, where to go next, and that the content they need is always one step away.

User persona & journey mapping
Sitemap & content hierarchy definition
Conversion funnel architecture
03

Design & Prototyping

High-fidelity interactive prototypes built in Figma — desktop, tablet, and mobile — so you can experience the product before it's built. Iterative design cycles with structured feedback rounds keep the process efficient and the outcome aligned.

Component-based design system creation
Interactive prototype for all key user flows
Dark mode, light mode & responsive variants
04

Testing, Handoff & Iteration

Usability testing with real users validates design decisions before development. Developer handoff includes complete design specifications, assets, and a component library. Post-launch A/B testing ensures continuous improvement.

Moderated & unmoderated usability testing
Developer handoff with design tokens & specs
Post-launch A/B testing & CRO programme
A Customised Strategy

Design Tailored to Your Audience

Your users are unique. Your brand is unique. Your UX strategy should be too — not adapted from a template but built from the ground up for your specific context.

Conversion Rate Optimisation

Systematic testing of headlines, CTAs, form flows, and page layouts to continuously improve the percentage of visitors who take the actions that matter — backed by statistical significance, not guesswork.

Design System Development

A reusable component library and design system that ensures visual consistency across your entire digital estate — and drastically reduces the cost of future design and development work.

Mobile-First Experience

Designed first for the smallest screen, then progressively enhanced for desktop — ensuring the majority of your Swiss users who browse on mobile have an exceptional experience.

Cross-Cultural Design

Design that works across Swiss linguistic and cultural contexts — from layout direction to colour associations and typographic preferences — for DE, FR, IT, and EN audiences.

Behavioural Analytics Integration

Heatmaps, session recordings, funnel analytics, and user surveys integrated from day one — so every future design decision is grounded in real user behaviour data.
