Website Development

High-Performance Websites
Built to Convert

Every pixel purposeful. Every millisecond matters. I build fast, beautiful, SEO-ready websites that turn visitors into customers — engineered for Swiss standards.

Our Objectives

What We Aim to Achieve

Every website project is guided by four non-negotiable goals that together produce a digital presence you can be proud of — and that works hard for your business.

Peak Performance

Core Web Vitals scores in the green. Sub-second load times on all devices. Speed is not a feature — it's the foundation every conversion is built on.

Conversion-First Design

Beautiful aesthetics paired with intentional UX flows. Every page has a clear purpose: guide the visitor toward the action that matters most to your business.

SEO-Ready from Day One

Semantic markup, structured data, clean architecture, and crawlable content — the technical foundation that lets search engines love your website immediately.

Scalable Architecture

Built to grow with you. Whether you expand your team, add new markets, or launch new products — the codebase and CMS can handle it without a rebuild.

Our Approach

How We Build Your Website

A structured, transparent process that keeps you informed at every stage and ensures the final product exceeds expectations.

01

Discovery & Strategy

We start with a deep-dive into your business goals, target audience, competitors, and existing digital presence. From this, I develop a clear site architecture, content plan, and technical specification — ensuring we build the right thing before writing a single line of code.

Stakeholder interviews & goal mapping
Competitor & market analysis
Sitemap & user journey definition
02

Design & Prototyping

I create high-fidelity wireframes and interactive prototypes before development begins. You can click through the experience, provide feedback, and sign off with full confidence — before a single line of production code is written.

Wireframes for all key templates
Interactive Figma prototype
Mobile & desktop design review cycles
03

Development & Integration

Clean, maintainable code built on a modern stack tailored to your needs — from static sites for maximum speed to headless CMS solutions for content teams. Integrations with your CRM, analytics, and third-party tools are built in from the start.

Semantic HTML5, CSS3 & modern JavaScript
CMS integration (Webflow, WordPress, Sanity, or custom)
Analytics, tracking & form integrations
04

Launch & Continuous Optimisation

Launch is not the end — it's the beginning. Post-launch, I monitor Core Web Vitals, user behaviour, and conversion rates, iterating based on real data to continuously improve performance and results.

Pre-launch QA across all browsers & devices
Performance audit & Core Web Vitals optimisation
30-day post-launch monitoring & iteration
