Real Estate Application

Tailored Proptech Platforms
That Close More Deals

Custom real estate applications built around the way Swiss buyers, sellers, and agents actually work — with AI-powered search, intelligent recommendations, and seamless lead flows.

Our Objectives

What We Aim to Achieve

A custom real estate application is only as valuable as the results it drives. These four objectives guide every decision in the build.

Powerful Property Search

Lightning-fast listing search with advanced filters — price, location, size, type, micro-neighbourhood — so buyers find the right property faster and stay on your platform longer.

Qualified Lead Generation

Built-in contact forms, appointment schedulers, and AI chatbots that capture, qualify, and route leads to the right agent — automatically, around the clock.

Live Market Intelligence

Integrated price indices, neighbourhood data, and market trend dashboards that position your platform as the definitive source of truth for Swiss real estate.

AI-Driven Personalisation

Machine-learning recommendation engines that surface the most relevant listings for each user, increasing time-on-site and the likelihood of a successful match.

Our Approach

How We Build Your Platform

Real estate applications have unique complexity. Our structured process manages that complexity while keeping delivery timelines tight and predictable.

01

Requirements & Architecture

We map every user persona — buyer, seller, agent, admin — and define the full feature set, data model, and integration requirements before architecture decisions are made. This prevents costly pivots later.

User persona & journey mapping
Data model & API architecture design
Third-party integration planning (IDX, MLS, CRM)
02

UI Design & Prototype

Intuitive, visually compelling interfaces for both the public-facing search experience and the agent/admin back-end. Every flow is prototyped and tested with real users before development.

Search, listing detail, and comparison page designs
Agent portal & admin dashboard design
Usability testing with target users
03

Full-Stack Development

Scalable backend with robust property database, performant search engine, and a polished frontend. Security, data privacy (GDPR/DSG), and performance are built into the architecture — not added afterwards.

High-performance property listing engine with geo-search
Secure user accounts, saved searches & alerts
Agent CRM integration & lead routing
04

Launch, Training & Growth

A phased launch strategy to ensure stability at scale, followed by team training and ongoing iteration. I monitor platform performance and implement improvements based on user behaviour data.

Phased rollout & load testing
Agent & admin team onboarding
Post-launch analytics & feature iteration
A Customised Strategy

Built Around Your Market

Whether you're a boutique agency in Geneva, a national portal, or a developer launching a project sales site — the platform is engineered to your specific context and ambitions.

Swiss Market Localisation

Four national languages, cantonal-level location data, Swiss property categories, and local regulatory compliance — handled natively in the platform architecture.

AI Recommendation Engine

Collaborative filtering models that learn from user behaviour to surface the most relevant listings — increasing engagement and reducing time-to-match for buyers and agents alike.

Mobile-First Experience

Over 70% of Swiss property searches begin on mobile. The application is built mobile-first, with touch-optimised maps, photo galleries, and contact flows.

CRM & Third-Party Integrations

Seamless connection to your existing CRM, email platform, WhatsApp Business, and data providers — so leads flow automatically and no opportunity is lost.

Security & Data Compliance

GDPR and Swiss DSG compliance by design. Role-based access control, encrypted user data, and regular security audits — protecting your clients and your reputation.
